Fettucine

Description

Fettuccine is a long, flat, and thick pasta that's commonly served with a creamy sauce. This classic Italian dish is simple to make and always delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley leaves, chopped

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the fettuccine and cook for 8-10 minutes, or until al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, melt the butter in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic to the saucepan and cook for 1-2 minutes, or until fragrant.
  4. Pour the heavy cream into the saucepan and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  5. Reduce the heat to low and simmer the sauce for 5-7 minutes, or until it has thickened slightly.
  6. Add the grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper to the saucepan. Stir until the cheese has melted and the sauce is smooth.
  7. Add the cooked fettuccine to the saucepan and toss it with the sauce until it's coated.
  8. Serve the fettuccine hot, garnished with chopped parsley leaves. Enjoy!
