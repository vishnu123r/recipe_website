Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish made with layers of pasta sheets, tomato sauce, cheese, and sometimes meat or vegetables. It's a hearty and flavorful comfort food that's perfect for feeding a crowd.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley leaves, chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles in a large pot of boiling salted water for 8-10 minutes, or until al dente. Drain the noodles and rinse them with cold water to prevent them from sticking together.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the ground beef or Italian sausage and cook until browned, breaking up any large chunks with a wooden spoon.
  4. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the onion is translucent.
  5. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir to combine and bring the sauce to a simmer. Cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  6. In a large bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, and parsley. Mix well.
  7. Spread a thin layer of the tomato sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Arrange a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce, slightly overlapping each noodle.
  9. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  10. Repeat the layering with sauce, noodles, and cheese mixture, ending with a layer of sauce on top.
  11. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella cheese over the top of the lasagna.
  12. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the lasagna is hot and bubbly.
  14. Let the lasagna cool for 5-10 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
