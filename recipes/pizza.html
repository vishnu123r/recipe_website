Pizza margherita

Description

Pizza margherita is a classic Italian dish that features a thin, crispy crust topped with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves. It's a simple yet delicious pizza that highlights the flavors of high-quality ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn or chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the pizza dough into a round shape, about 12-14 inches in diameter.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the pizza dough, leaving about 1/2 inch around the edges.
  5. Arrange the sliced mozzarella cheese on top of the tomato sauce.
  6. Drizzle a little bit of olive oil over the cheese and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  9. Sprinkle fresh basil leaves over the top of the pizza before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
